Admission

Thank you for your interest in College Government Polytechnic Awasari. We offer various diploma courses in technical fields. Below are the general admission requirements:

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed high school education or equivalent
  • Minimum aggregate score of XX%
  • Proficiency in English and mathematics
  • Additional requirements specific to each program (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online application form with accurate information.
  2. Attach the required documents, such as academic transcripts, certificates, and identification proof.
  3. Submit the application and pay the application fee.
  4. Once the application is reviewed, you will be notified of the admission decision.
  5. If accepted, follow the instructions provided to complete the enrollment process.
